Development dashboard shows the upcoming releases of all the XL Platform products. It is a snapshot of currently planned development activities updated on a daily level.

To view the upcoming releases please select the product of your interest. The dashboard showcases planned major and maintenance releases for the select product and the XebiaLabs supported plugins related to the product.

The list of issues can be filtered by the type of release, the issue number (e.g. DEPL-3467) or any arbitrary text.
